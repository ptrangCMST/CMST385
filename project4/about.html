JPPSO or Joint Personal Property Shiping Office, is a DOD United States of Air Force unit. The goal of JPPSO is to assist, perform, lead service members in the right direction during their permanent change of station. The goal is to ensure member's personal goods, such as furniture, clothes, special household items, are delivered at their new duty assignment successfuly. JPPSO is managed by the major command, AFDW or Air Force District of Washington. The major command oversees multiple Air Force special duty units, to include JPPSO. There are multiple major JPPSO office, JPPSO-NC, JPPSO-NE, JPPSO-SC, JPPSO-SE, JPPSO-NW, and JPPSO-SW. The locations are in charged of certain areas of responsibiltiy within the CONUS, Continental United States. JPPSO-NC is the biggest division, over 25 states within their respective AOR, with their main hub located in Colorado Springs, Colorado.
Within JPPSO, there are divisions, and within those divisions are sections. See the table below for the break down of the sections.
